POCKET WATCH


Waltham Grade 620 Sterling Silver Hunter Case Pocket Watch 1899.
As above, a sterling silver cased Waltham 620 pocket watch.
Runs well and seems to keep time, although I can't make any guarantees - like all vintage watches it'd be worth servicing it for the best performance.
The movement is a beautiful Waltham grade 620, size 16s, serial number 9238268 which dates to 1899 according to the online registries.
The case is a full hunter style, stamped as Sterling silver, and made by Benedict Brothers, 171 Broadway, New York, with serial number 843.
I believe it is missing glass over the dial, although I'm not 100% certain whether it should have had that originally.
It's in good condition and would polish up well.
The dial is an almost perfect enamelled example, with a couple of small blemishes that may clean up, Roman numerals and a running subseconds at 6.
This is also signed "Benedict Brothers New York".
The hands are beautifully delicate blued steel, although have tarnished somewhat
